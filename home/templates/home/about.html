About

FitJacket helps users stay active! With FitJacket, users can track their fitness progress and get personalized workout recommendations. FitJacket provides an Ai-powered personalized fitness experience that adapts to user goals and progress.

To motivate users, FitJacket includes social features where users can connect with friends, join challenges, and celebrate achievements. The platform also integrates fitness tracking APIs to sync workout data from third-party apps and devices, to make tracking more convenient.
